Seeing drivers in a new light

We partnered with Uber to create a site that will house documentary-style short films that tell the story of Uber drivers. Opting for a dark interface to showcase stunning portraits and prioritize content, we've crafted a straightforward design with smooth page transitions for an outstanding user experience. This gave Uber the essential tools to effortlessly update content and amplify the voices of Uber drivers.
