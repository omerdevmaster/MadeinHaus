Motion Graphics Artist

Full-time, Los AngelesFull-time, Los Angeles

Haus is looking for an experienced Motion Graphics Artist to join our team. In this role, you would be responsible for collaborating with our team to create stunning and effective original design and animation for a wide array of marketing assets and applications, including but not limited to animated mockups, explainer animations, trailers, announcements, social content, and more.

Apply now

RequirementsRequirements

An ideal candidate will be comfortable designing style frames informed by creative briefs/mood boards and executing fully animated treatments for use in a digital setting (bonus points if you dabble in character animation).

We're looking for a detail-oriented individual who is experienced in managing more than one concurrent project in a high-speed, high-performance environment.

Reporting to Creative Directors, Art Directors and/or Motion Leads you will join a team that creates a wide array of marketing assets. You will support initiatives lead by our internal creative team as well as external partners and clients. You may be asked to collaborate with other GFX artists, editors, and producers on creative ideation and exploration in executing new designs and animation.

  • 4+ years experience as an advanced Motion Graphics Artist
  • Strong design, typographic and animation skills a must!
  • Strong knowledge of current animation and motion tools including the Adobe Suite
  • Experience with designing original motion graphics from conceptual creative direction.
  • 3d experience is a big plus but not required
  • Strong communication skills
  • Attention to detail, demonstrating a high degree of accuracy and consistency.
  • Self managing / Self-starter, with an ability to work autonomously offsite or onsite.
  • Excellent organization and time management; able to prioritize large volume of work effectively and efficiently while hitting tight deadlines.
  • Team player willing to jump in where needed in order to create top of the line assets.
  • Contribute to a diverse, vibrant team dynamic while providing a personal perspective on creative.

Personality Attributes

  • You are passionate and curious about all aspects of motion graphic design with an entrepreneurial mindset.
  • You thrive in a fast-paced and demanding environment, possess a high level of intellectual curiosity, and demonstrate strong judgment in the face of ambiguity.
  • You are an agile, goal-oriented personality, driven to succeed and always up for a challenge, and most importantly able to self-manage.
  • You are a team player and collaborator, willing to jump into a project when needed in order to create top of the line assets while working alongside fellow top of the line creatives.

ONLY ONLINE SUBMISSIONS WILL BE CONSIDERED. PLEASE DO NOT CALL OR REACH OUT TO ANYONE DIRECTLY.
